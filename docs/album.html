Tracklist

Discover the song that bring the album to life

  1. Whispers at the Temple Fair: A song inspired by the mystical echoes of temple bells.
  2. Dancing Under the Banyan Tree: Celebrating life with playful, rhythmic beats.
  3. Lanterns of Liberation: The story of freedom told through soft, glowing melodies.
  4. Golden Rice Fields: A tribute to the beauty of harvest under the golden sun.
  5. Threads of Silk and Sweat: Interweaving stories of craftsmanship and perseverance.
  6. Echoes of the Drum: A deep and powerful rhythm inspired by cultural festivities.
  7. Her Voice in the Wind: A haunting yet beautiful call carried by the breeze.
  8. Rain Song of the Village: Gentle rainfalls paired with soothing harmonies.
  9. Freedom on a Bamboo Stage: A vibrant tale of performance and liberation.
  10. Blossoms Beneath the Moon: The title track, capturing the essence of serenity and wonder.
